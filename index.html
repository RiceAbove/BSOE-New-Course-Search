BSOE New Course Search

Starting Fall 2019, many BSOE Courses will have changed course numbers and codes. The content of courses will not be changing, with the exception of the new introductory Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) programming sequence. This website is to help fellow slugs quickly find new courses without having to comb through this. This is still a work in progress so if you notice anything wrong or weird happening, please contact me or if you're familiar with github, click here.
